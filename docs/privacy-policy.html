Privacy Policy for Sid's Word of the Day

Last updated: 2/2/2026
Developer: Lachlan Maclean
App: Sid's Word of the Day ("the App")

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Sid's Word ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application on Android.

Information We Collect

Sid's Word is designed to use minimal information:

  • Local data: The App may store preferences on your device (for example, your daily reminder time) so the App can work as you expect. This data stays on your device.
  • Device information: To show notifications (e.g. daily reminder), the App uses your device's notification and system time. We do not collect or send this information to our servers.

We do not collect your name, email, or other personally identifiable information unless you explicitly provide it (e.g. through a future feedback form) and agree to its use.

How We Use Information

  • To provide the App's features (e.g. Word of the Day, daily reminder).
  • To improve the App and fix issues, where applicable.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage and Security

  • Preference data (such as reminder time) is stored only on your device.
  • You can clear this data by clearing the App's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services (e.g. Google Play services) that have their own privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies. We do not control data collected by those services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will work to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy in the App or on the Play Store listing, with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
